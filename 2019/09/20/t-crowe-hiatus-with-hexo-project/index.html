T. Crowe Hiatus with Hexo Project

I sent a message to the hexo core team but I also wanted to mention here that I am taking a break from hexo. I am focusing on building youreadfor.me which is a hugely important step to open up audiobook publishing for everyone.

If you’re not familiar with hexo it’s an excellent way to build static sites. Static sites simplify website publishing and they are secure and portable across many different services.

I’m very pleased with hexo and I’ll continue to use it in my free time. This blog and theme are created with hexo. And when there is more time in the future I will help again.

Thank you hexo team and see you in the future! 👋🏻
